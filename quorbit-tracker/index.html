Product Objective
A matching intelligence layer for Indian SMBs and startups. Companies post jobs and import candidates sourced from LinkedIn, Apollo, and Naukri into a unified dashboard — Quorbit auto-scores each candidate (0–100) against the job fingerprint. Top-scoring candidates receive an AI chat conversation that assesses job readiness. Candidate self-signup is secondary — companies bring their own pipeline first. Long-term moat: LLM conversation history (ChatGPT/Claude exports) becomes a passive ambient signal layer no other platform has.
🏢 Business-side first 💰 Companies pay · Candidates free 🤖 Score → Chat → Hire 🔒 LLM conversation = the moat
